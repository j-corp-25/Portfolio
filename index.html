About Me

Hello! I'm a seasoned professional who initially established roots in the investment management industry, specializing in operations and accounting. With an expansive background, I ensured the smooth functioning of financial processes and championed efficiency in my role. My aptitude for data analysis and modeling is underlined by my proficiency in Microsoft Excel. A passion for automation led me to delve deep into macros, optimizing performance wherever possible. Recognizing the immense potential of technology and software, I transitioned into software development. A soon-to-be graduate from App Academy, I've honed skills in CSS-HTML, Rails, Ruby, MongoDB, React, and JavaScript, marrying my finance background with coding to bring a unique perspective to problem-solving.
